## 引言

本文件规定 classes of 光伏 (PV) 性能监测系统（性能监测系统（performance 监测系统）s） and serves as guidance for 监测系统 choices.

图 1 illustrates major elements comprising different PV 系统类型. The main clauses of this document are written for 并网系统（grid-connected systems） without 本地负载（本地负载s）, 储能（energy 储能）, or 辅助电源（辅助电源s）, as shown by the bold lines in 图 1. Annex E includes some details for systems with additional components.

The PV 阵列 may include both 固定轴（fixed-axis） and 跟踪支架系统（tracker systems） and both 平板式（flat-plate） and 聚光系统.

![图 1 – PV 系统的可能组成要素](./images/PV-system.svg)

**图例**

RNE: 可再生能源（renewable energy）
PCE: 功率调节设备（power conditioning equipment）
BDI: 双向逆变器（bi-directional inverter）
GCI: 并网逆变器（grid-connected inverter）

粗线表示不含本地负载、储能或辅助电源的简单并网系统。

The purposes of a 性能监测系统（performance 监测系统） are diverse and could include comparing performance to 设计预期 and 保证值 以及 故障检测和定位.

For comparing performance to 设计预期 and 保证值, the focus 宜 on 系统级数据 and consistency between 预测方法和测试方法.

For 故障检测和定位 there 宜 greater resolution at 系统子级 and an emphasis on 测量重复性（measurement repeatability） and 相关性指标（correlation metrics）.

The 监测系统 宜 adapted to the PV 系统's size and 用户要求. In general, larger PV 系统 宜具有 more 监测点 and 更高准确度的传感器 than smaller and lower-cost PV 系统.

### 3.2 采样间隔（sampling interval）

样本之间的时间

### 3.3 记录（record）

被记录并存储的数据

### 3.4 记录间隔（recording interval）

τ

记录之间的时间

### 3.5 报告（report）

基于一系列记录的聚合值

### 3.6 报告周期（reporting period）

报告之间的时间

### 3.7 正面（front side）

表面通常朝向天空的一侧

### 3.8 背面（rear side）

表面通常朝向地面的一侧

### 3.9 单面 PV 器件（monofacial PV device）

PV 器件 in which only the front side is 用于发电

### 3.10 双面 PV 器件（bifacial PV device）

PV 器件 in which 正面和背面两侧 are 用于发电

### 3.11 双面率系数（bifaciality coefficient）

φ

之间的比值 an I-V 特性（current-voltage characteristic） of the rear side and the front side of a 双面器件（bifacial device）, typically at 标准测试条件（Standard Test Conditions，STC） (STC), unless otherwise specified

注 1 to entry: Bifaciality coefficients include the 短路电流（short-circuit current） bifaciality coefficient $\varphi_{\mathrm{Isc}}$ , the 开路电压（open-circuit voltage） bifaciality coefficient $\varphi_{\mathrm{Voc}}$ and the 最大功率（maximum power） bifaciality coefficient $\varphi_{\mathrm{Pmax}}$ .

注 2 to entry: Bifaciality coefficients 被定义 in IEC TS 60904-1-2.

### 3.12 辐照度（irradiance）

G

单位面积入射辐射功率通量

注 1 to entry: 单位表示为 W·m $^{-2}$ .

### 3.13 阵列面内辐照度（in-plane irradiance）

$G_{i}$ or POA

sum of 直射、散射和地面反射辐照度 incident upon the front side of an 倾斜表面 与组件平面平行 in the PV 阵列, also known as 阵列平面（plane-of-array，POA） (POA) irradiance

注 1 to entry: 单位表示为 W·m $^{-2}$ .

### 3.14 水平反照率（horizontal albedo）

$\rho_{H}$

proportion of incident light reflected by a 地表 as measured in a horizontal plane

注 1 to entry: 它是 property of a 地表 and is a 无量纲量 on a scale from 0 to 1.

### 3.15 阵列面内背面辐照度比（in-plane rear-side irradiance ratio）

$\rho_{i}$

ratio of the irradiance incident on the rear side of the modules in the PV 阵列 to the irradiance incident on the front side

注 1 to entry: 它是 无量纲量 but can exceed a value of 1 since, 除……之外 reflected light, diffuse and direct components of the solar resource may also be measured on the 背面 of the plane of array.

### 3.16 光谱匹配的阵列面内背面辐照度比（spectrally matched in-plane rear-side irradiance ratio）

$\rho_{i}^{SP}$

in-plane 背面 irradiance ratio per 3.15 when both irradiance quantities 被测量 with a spectrally matched reference device or with the application of 光谱修正因子（spectral correction factors） per IEC 60904-7

### 3.17 光谱匹配参考器件（spectrally matched reference device）

reference device 例如 a PV 电池片 or module with 光谱响应特性（spectral response characteristics） sufficiently close to those of the PV 组件 in the PV 阵列 such that 光谱失配误差（spectral mismatch errors） are small under the 典型入射光谱范围

### 3.18 阵列面内背面辐照度（in-plane rear-side irradiance）

$G_{i}^{rear}$ or POA $^{rear}$

sum of 直射、散射和地面反射辐照度 incident on the rear side of the modules in the PV 阵列, also known as 背面 阵列平面（plane-of-array，POA） irradiance

注 1 to entry: 单位表示为 W·m $^{-2}$ .

注 2 to entry: (If measured via in-plane 背面 irradiance ratio): $G_{i}^{rear} = \rho_{i} \times G_{i}$ or $G_{i,SP}^{rear} = \rho_{i}^{SP} \times G_{i}$ .

### 3.19 双面参考器件（bifacial reference device）

bifacial PV 器件, 例如 a 电池片 or module, having substantially the same properties, 相对于 response to 正面 and 背面 irradiance, as 双面组件（bifacial modules） to be monitored

### 3.20 双面辐照度因子（bifacial irradiance factor，BIF）

BIF

dimensionless factor that 可 directly multiplied by the 正面 in-plane irradiance ( $G_{i}$ ) to calculate the “effective” irradiance reaching a 双面器件（bifacial device） from both the front and rear side collectively

注 1 to entry: $BIF = (1 + \varphi_{Pmax} \times \rho_t)$ or $BIF^{sp} = (1 + \varphi_{Pmax} \times \rho_t^{sp})$ . See 3.11, 3.15, 3.16.

注 2 to entry: Rear-side POA 辐照度 可 measured simultaneously with 正面 POA 辐照度 using a 双面参考器件（bifacial reference device）. In that case, $BIF = G_{i}^{BIFI Ref Device} \div G_{i}$ . For consistency, the 正面 POA 辐照度 宜 measured with the same or similar type of device as the 双面参考器件（bifacial reference device）.

注 3 to entry: "Effective" irradiance may include the effect of 不均匀性（inhomogeneities） in 背面 irradiance.

### 3.21 全球水平辐照度（global horizontal irradiance，GHI）

GHI

direct plus diffuse irradiance incident on the front side of a horizontal surface

注 1 to entry: 单位表示为 W·m $^{-2}$ .

注 2 to entry: $GHI = DNI \cdot \cos Z + DHI$ where $Z$ is the 太阳天顶角（solar zenith angle）.

### 3.22 日周区域（circumsolar）

immediately surrounding the 太阳圆盘（solar disk）

### 3.23 直接法向辐照度（direct normal irradiance，DNI）

DNI

irradiance emanating from the 太阳圆盘（solar disk） and from the 日周区域（circumsolar region） of the sky within a 所张全角 of $5^{\circ}$ falling on a plane surface normal to the sun's rays

注 1 to entry: 单位表示为 W·m $^{-2}$ .

注 2 to entry: $GHI = DNI \cdot \cos Z + DHI$ where $Z$ is the 太阳天顶角（solar zenith angle）.

### 3.24 日周贡献（circumsolar contribution）

contribution of a specific portion of the circumsolar normal irradiance to the 直接法向辐照度（direct normal irradiance，DNI）. The circumsolar contribution refers to a specific ring-shaped angular region described by an inner and the outer angular distance from the centre of the sun (see ISO 9488)

注 1 to entry: If the inner angle describing this angular region is the half-angle of the sun disk the circumsolar contribution is also called circumsolar ratio.

注 2 to entry: Depending on the circumsolar irradiance measurement instrument or the solar technology involved, different wavelength ranges are included. In order to describe circumsolar irradiance correctly, the wavelength range or the spectral response of the instrument or the involved technology has to be specified.

### 3.25 日周比（circumsolar ratio）

fraction of measured 直接法向辐照度（direct normal irradiance，DNI） (DNI) emanating from the 日周区域（circumsolar region） of the sky, i.e. within the 角接收范围（angular acceptance） of the DNI sensor but outside the 太阳圆盘（solar disk）

### 3.26 太阳形状（sunshape）

方位平均辐亮度分布（azimuthal average radiance profile） as a function of the angular distance from the centre of the sun, normalized to 1 at the centre of the sun and considering the wavelength range of 短波辐射（shortwave radiation） (see ISO 9488)

### 3.27 散射水平辐照度（diffuse horizontal irradiance，DHI）

$G_{d}$ or DHI

global irradiance on the front side of a horizontal surface excluding the portion emanating from the 太阳圆盘（solar disk） and from the 日周区域（circumsolar region） of the sky within a 所张全角 of $5^{\circ}$

注 1 to entry: 单位表示为 W·m $^{-2}$ .

注 2 to entry: $GHI = DNI \cdot \cos Z + DHI$ where $Z$ is the 太阳天顶角（solar zenith angle）.

### 3.28 阵列面内直射束辐照度（in-plane direct beam irradiance）

$G_{i,b}$

in-plane irradiance incident upon the front side of an 倾斜表面 与组件平面平行 in the PV 阵列 emanating from the 太阳圆盘（solar disk） and from the 日周区域（circumsolar region） of the sky within a 所张全角 of $5^{\circ}$

注 1 to entry: The in-plane 直射束辐照度（直射束 irradiance） $G_{i,b} = \cos(\theta) \times DNI$ , where $\theta$ is the angle between the sun and the normal to the plane. When the plane of array is normal to the sun, $G_{i,b} = DNI$ .

注 2 to entry: 单位表示为 W·m $^{-2}$ .

### 3.29 辐照量（irradiation）

11

irradiance integrated over a 指定时间间隔

注 1 to entry: 单位表示为 kW·h·m $^{-2}$ .

### 3.30 标准测试条件（standard test conditions，STC）

STC

in-plane irradiance 1000 W·m $^{-2}$ , normal incidence, PV 电池结温 25 °C, and the 参考光谱辐照度 defined in IEC 60904-3

### 3.31 污损比（soiling ratio，SR）

SR

ratio of the 实际功率输出 of the PV 阵列 under given soiling conditions to the power that would be expected if the PV 阵列 were 清洁且无污损

### 3.32 污损水平（soiling level，SL）

SL

分数功率损失 due to soiling, given by 1 - SR

### 3.33 污损速率（soiling rate）

变化率 of soiling ratio, typically expressed in percent per day

### 3.34 有功功率（active power）

P

under 周期条件, mean value, taken over one period, of the 电流和电压瞬时乘积

注 1 to entry: Under 正弦条件, the 有功功率（active power） is the 复功率的实部.

注 2 to entry: 单位表示为 W.

### 3.35 视在功率（apparent power）

S

product of the 均方根电压（rms voltage） between the terminals of a two-terminal element or two-terminal circuit and the 均方根电流（rms electric current） in the element or circuit

注 1 to entry: Under 正弦条件, the 视在功率（apparent power） is the modulus of the complex power.

注 2 to entry: 单位表示为 VA.

### 3.36 功率因数（power factor）

λ

under 周期条件, ratio of the absolute value of the 有功功率（active power） P to the 视在功率（apparent power） S:

$$
\lambda = \frac {| P |}{S}
$$

## 4 监测系统分类

本文件规定 two classifications of 监测系统, A 类 and B 类.

A 类 适用于 大型 PV 系统 例如 公用事业规模（utility-scale） or large 商业安装.

B 类 适用于 smaller systems 例如 屋顶式 or 小到中型 商业安装.

Users of the document may specify whichever classification is most appropriate to their application, regardless of PV 系统规模.

The 监测系统 classification 应 stated in any 符合性声明 to this document.

Throughout this document, some requirements are designated as 适用于特定分类. Where no designation is given, the requirements apply to 两个分类.

## 5 总则

### 5.1 测量精密度和不确定度

测量精密度 refers to 重复性和分辨率, which have the meanings defined in the IEC Electropedia.

测量不确定度 refers to accuracy and otherwise has the meaning defined in the IEC Electropedia.

测量不确定度 可 calculated as outlined in ISO/IEC Guide 98-1 and ISO/IEC Guide 98-3.

### 5.2 校准

Recalibration of 传感器和信号调理电子设备 应 performed as 推荐 by the 制造商 or at more frequent intervals 在有规定时 in the standard.

建议 perform 定期交叉检查 of each sensor against 同类传感器（sister sensors） or 参考器件（reference devices） 为了 identify 失准传感器.

### 5.3 重复要素

Depending on 系统规模 and 用户要求, the 监测系统 may include 传感器冗余 and/or 传感器要素重复配置 for different components or 分区 of the full PV 系统. Accordingly, the measured and 计算参数 defined in this document may have multiple instances, each corresponding to a subsection or 子部件 of the PV 系统.

### 5.4 功率消耗

The 寄生功率（parasitic power） drawn by any systems required for operation of the PV 电站 不应 be considered as a load supplied by the plant.

### 5.5 文档记录

Details of all components of the 监测系统 应 documented. All system 检查和维护, including 清洁, 应 documented.

### 5.6 检查

The 监测系统 应 inspected 至少每年一次 and preferably at more frequent intervals. Inspection should look for damage, deterioration, or disconnection of sensors and electrical enclosures, soiling or displacement of optical sensors, 接线松动, 温度传感器脱落, 附件脆化, and other potential problems.

## 6 数据采集时序和报告

### 6.1 样本、记录和报告

图 2 illustrates the relations between samples, records, and reports.

A sample is 从传感器或测量装置获取的数据. The 采样间隔（sampling interval） is the 样本之间的时间. Samples do not need to be 永久存储.

A record is data entered into 数据存储, 基于 acquired samples. The 记录间隔（recording interval）, denoted by $\tau$ in this document, is the 记录之间的时间. The 记录间隔（recording interval） 宜 an 整数倍 of the 采样间隔（sampling interval）, and an integer number of 记录间隔（recording interval）s should fit within $1\mathrm{h}$ .

The 记录的参数值 for each record is the 平均值、最大值、最小值、总和, or other function of the samples acquired during the 记录间隔（recording interval）, as appropriate for the measured quantity. The record can also include 补充数据 例如 additional statistics of the samples, number of 缺失数据点, 错误代码, 暂态（transients）, and/or other data of 特别关注. (For wind data records, see statement in 9.3.)

A report is an aggregate value covering multiple 记录间隔（recording interval）s. The 报告周期（reporting period） is the 报告之间的时间. Typically the 报告周期（reporting period） would be chosen to be days, weeks, months, or years.

Each record and each report 应包括 a 时间戳（timestamp）.

## 8 辐照度（irradiance）

### 8.1 传感器类型

Approaches to ground-基于-site irradiance measurement include:

- Measure total broadband hemispherical solar irradiance, independent of spectral or angular distribution. Instruments with this objective are classified as 总辐射表（总辐射表（pyranometer）s）, regardless of technology type.
- Measure matched irradiance corresponding to the PV-usable portion of the incident spectral and angular distribution. PV 参考器件（PV reference device）s (reference 电池片s and modules) 用于 this objective.
- Measure spectral irradiance, from which 光谱匹配辐照度 可 determined. Spectroradiometers or 多光谱仪器（multi-spectral instrument）s 可 used for this objective.

Measurements 可 transposed between approaches using appropriate models, with some uncertainty. If used, model-based transpositions and corrections 应 documented.

Irradiance may also be determined from remote measurements using satellite instrumentation as a supplement, or replacement (when permitted by 表 2), for ground-基于-site measurements. See 8.3.12.

The selected sensor and sensor type 应 documented.

### 8.2 通用要求

#### 8.2.1 概述

This subclause 8.2 provides general requirements applicable to most on-site irradiance measurements. Subsequent subclauses on specific irradiance measurement types may include different and/or additional requirements.

#### 8.2.3 传感器位置

##### 8.2.3.1 正面

The location of 正面 irradiance measurement sensors, including GHI and 阵列平面（plane-of-array，POA） sensors, 应 chosen to be representative and to avoid 遮挡条件 from 从日出到日落, if possible. Shading should only occur within a half hour of sunrise or sunset and any shading 应 documented.

For 正面 阵列平面（plane-of-array，POA） irradiance measurements, for either 固定倾角（fixed-tilt） or 跟踪系统, sensors 应 maintained at the same 倾角和方位角 as the modules. This 可 achieved by placing the sensors either directly on the 组件支架（module racking） or on separate poles or 延伸臂 with tracking, 如适用.

注 选项ally, additional 正面 irradiance sensors 可 placed in locations that are temporarily shaded by adjacent module rows, e.g. during 反跟踪（backtracking） of a tracking system, 为了 monitor this shading effect, but these sensors do not satisfy the requirements of 表 2 and 表 3 and the 性能指标 always use unshaded sensors, unless explicitly noted.

##### 8.2.3.2 背面

The location of 背面 irradiance and/or in-plane 背面 irradiance ratio sensors 应 chosen such that they have a 视场（field of view） representative of the conditions present on the rear side in the 阵列大部分区域, while minimizing shading on the modules. If the expected 地表 varies throughout the site, use an appropriate quantity of sensors and sampling methodology to capture the variations. The sensors should also be placed to capture the 背面 irradiance without impact from local surroundings other than representative shading by nearby portions of the PV 阵列.

The sensors 宜 placed at the same 倾角 as the modules, directly on the 组件支架（module racking）, by using beam or rail support structures, and positioned away from row ends, mounting piers, and other sources of 局部遮挡 or 增强照明 phenomena 例如 reflections from the 组件支架（module racking）.

A concern regarding in-plane 背面 irradiance sensors and in-plane 背面 irradiance ratio sensors is the 非均匀性（non-uniformity） of the irradiance reaching the back side of the module surface from edge to edge. 建议 place multiple sensors along the rear side of the racking structure to follow and measure the non-uniform 照明分布（illumination profile） throughout the day. This allows both quantifying the 非均匀性（non-uniformity） of the 背面 irradiance and calculating an 有效平均值 of the 背面 irradiance for introduction into selected performance formulas.

注 The measured irradiance may differ depending on the position of the sensor, especially in the case of 背面 POA sensor measurements. For example, if the sensor is placed below a row of modules, it may show a different reading than when placed above the row of modules, since a contribution to the irradiance in a tilted plane originates from the ground or nearby features.

##### 8.2.3.3 水平反照率（horizontal albedo）

The location of 水平反照率传感器 应 chosen to be representative of the albedo at the site. The sensors 宜 mounted at a minimum height of 1,0 m 为了 allow for a sufficient 视场（field of view） of irradiance reflecting from the ground and 不宜 be shaded by vegetation or any nearby structures, including modules and the module support structure, within a ±80 degree 视角（viewing angle）. Shading by the albedo measurement device and its support structure 宜 minimized. If the expected 地表 varies throughout the site, use an appropriate quantity of sensors and sampling methodology to capture the variations.

#### 8.2.4 重新校准

Recalibration of sensors 应 conducted in a manner that minimizes downtime and 传感器停用 为了 prevent interruption of monitoring. Effective 方法 may include:

- Exchanging 已安装传感器 with new or 重新校准的单元
• Performing on-site 重新校准 of sensors 在可能时
• Providing redundant sensors and alternating 实验室重新校准计划.

For A 类 systems, sensors 应 recalibrated once every 2 years, or more frequently 按照制造商建议.

For B 类 systems, recalibrate sensors 根据 制造商 recommendations.

#### 8.2.5 污损缓解

For A 类 systems the effects of 污损积累 on irradiance sensors 应 mitigated. For typical sensors and installations, 每周清洁 是必需的. Cleaning 可 employed less frequently when local conditions allow or when technology is employed which mitigates or corrects for sensor soiling equivalently to 每周清洁 or detects soiling so that 清洁 可 scheduled when needed.

#### 8.2.6 露水和霜冻缓解

For A 类 systems, the effects of 露水和霜冻 accumulation on irradiance sensors 应 mitigated for locations where dew or frost is expected during more than 2 % of annual GHI-hours.

Determination of whether an installation site requires mitigation 可 performed by examining typical 典型气象年数据 for the site, paying attention to 环境温度 and 露点（dew point）. For the purposes of this assessment, dew or frost is considered expected when 环境温度 is within 1,5 °C of 露点（dew point）.

Various means of mitigation, including heating and 外部通风, 可 effective. Irradiance sensors shall maintain their accuracy and classification while 露水和霜冻 mitigation is applied. Heating 不应 disturb the sensor's accuracy and classification. For 总辐射表（总辐射表（pyranometer）s）, effective means of ensuring accurate performance while the sensor is heated may include, but are not limited to, 内部和外部通风.

#### 8.2.7 检查和维护

Routine inspection of sensors 应 performed to check for soiling, 未对准, and other fault conditions. For A 类 systems, 正面 sensors 应 inspected weekly.

Sensors 应 maintained 根据 制造商 requirements. Maintenance requirements may include, 例如, 干燥剂检查 and/or replacement, where applicable.

#### 8.2.8 传感器对准

Irradiance sensors for 全球水平辐照度（global horizontal irradiance，GHI） (GHI) 应 调平 to within 0,5°.

Irradiance sensors for 阵列平面（plane-of-array，POA） (POA) irradiance 应 aligned with their 目标平面 within $0,5^{\circ}$ of tilt and $1^{\circ}$ of azimuth (A 类) or $1^{\circ}$ of tilt and $2^{\circ}$ of azimuth (B 类), with the following provisions:

- When the sensors are placed directly on the 组件支架（module racking）, the alignment requirement is met if it 可 shown that the sensors are aligned to the racking to within the stated tolerances.
- When the sensors are placed on another mounting structure independent of the modules, care 应 taken to achieve and verify alignment to within stated tolerances. If alignment cannot be achieved, the alignment error 应 measured and documented.

注 Sensor tilt 可 measured with an 倾角仪（inclinometer）. Azimuthal alignment of 阵列平面（plane-of-array，POA） sensors 可 verified by reviewing and modelling a time series of irradiance data in 晴空条件.

#### 8.3.8 光谱匹配辐照度

For determination of the usable solar resource, 可选 spectral matching of irradiance measurements for the user's specific PV 组件 宜 considered.

Spectrally matched 背面 irradiance is particularly relevant because the spectrum of ground-reflected radiation can differ significantly from the incident solar radiation.

Methods for determining 光谱匹配辐照度 include:

- Measuring 光谱匹配辐照度 using a spectrally matched reference device per 3.17. The residual spectral mismatch 可 determined by IEC 60904-7 considering typical spectra for the application. Users should select a degree of residual spectral mismatch appropriate to their application. Identical PV technology is not necessarily required; 例如, commercial monocrystalline silicon reference 电池片s will provide beneficial spectral matching (compared to broadband measurement) for most commercial crystalline silicon PV technologies.
- Measuring broadband or non-光谱匹配辐照度, e.g. using a 总辐射表（pyranometer）, plus performing a 基于模型的光谱失配修正 using 环境数据 例如 temperature, 湿度, etc.
- Measuring spectral irradiance, e.g. using a 光谱辐射计（spectroradiometer） or other 多光谱仪器（multi-spectral instrument）, from which 光谱修正因子（spectral correction factors） 可 derived and applied to broadband irradiance data to obtain 光谱匹配辐照度.

#### 8.3.9 聚光系统的阵列面内辐照度

For 聚光系统, the total in-plane irradiance is replaced by the irradiance captured by the 聚光器（concentrator）.

\- For 聚光系统 that capture only the 直射束:

The in-plane irradiance $G_{i}$ is replaced by the in-plane 直射束辐照度（直射束 irradiance） $G_{i,b}$ :

$$
G _ {\mathrm{i}} = G _ {\mathrm{i,b}} \tag {1}
$$

\- For 聚光系统 that capture some diffuse light 除……之外 the 直射束: The in-plane irradiance is replaced by the 有效辐照度（effective irradiance） ( $G_{\text{eff}}$ ) owing to 部分散射光捕获, where the fraction of diffuse light is quantified by the parameter $f_{\text{d}}$ :

$$
G _ {\mathrm{i}} \rightarrow G _ {\mathrm{eff}} = (G _ {\mathrm{i,b}} + f _ {\mathrm{d}} \cdot (G _ {\mathrm{i}} - G _ {\mathrm{i,b}})) \tag {2}
$$

Determination of $f_{d}$ begins by obtaining full current and voltage characteristics of a CPV 组件 over many days with varying fractions of diffuse energy; a clear day will have little diffuse energy while a cloudy day will provide mainly diffuse energy. Analysis of a 散射比例（diffuse fraction） for a given low and medium concentration CPV 组件 宜 based upon a large number of I-V 曲线 where global in-plane irradiance ( $G_{i}$ ) is above $21\ W\cdot m^{-2}$ .

A fundamental premise of this method is that the 短路电流（short-circuit current） ( $I_{sc}$ ) 可 consistently and reliably estimated by acquiring a full trace of the current-voltage ( $I-V$ ) curve for the 被测器件（device under test，DUT） (DUT) and that the 温度系数（temperature coefficient） for the $I_{sc}$ parameter of the DUT has been well characterized in advance. When this premise is valid, the diffuse light capture characterization of a CPV 组件 or receiver becomes simply a matter of determining the 短路电流（short-circuit current）, $I_{sc,0}$ normalized to standard test conditions (STC) and then relating the as-measured $I_{sc,0}$ to this reference using an "有效辐照度（effective irradiance）" $G_{\text{eff}}$ , 例如 that shown in Formula (2). One significant advantage of this approach is that compensating for the effects of solar spectrum 可 accomplished by adjusting only the $I_{sc}$ parameter.

By plotting the terms on the left-hand side of Formula (3) on the y-axis of a 2D graph and by plotting $G_{i,b}/G_{i}$ on the x-axis, the 斜率和截距 可 easily determined from the form $y = mx + b$ after performing a 线性回归分析（linear regression analysis） of the $I_{sc}$ vs. $G_{i,b}/G_{i}$ data.

$$
\frac {1 0 0 0 \mathrm{W} \cdot \mathrm{m} ^ {- 2}}{G _ {i}} \times \frac {I _ {s c}}{\left[ 1 + \alpha_ {I _ {s c}} \times (T _ {c} - 2 5 ^ {\circ} \mathrm{C}) \right]} = \left(I _ {s c, 0} \times f _ {d}\right) + \left(\frac {G _ {i , b}}{G _ {i}}\right) \times \left(I _ {s c, 0} - f _ {d} \times I _ {s c, 0}\right) \tag {3}
$$

where

$\alpha_{Isc}$ is the 温度系数（temperature coefficient） for $I_{sc}$ ,

$T_{c}$ is the 电池片 temperature in °C,

$I_{sc,0}$ is the short circuit current at STC and $0^{\circ}$ 入射角（angle of incidence）.

The term $f_{d}$ then becomes:

$$
f _ {d} = \frac {b}{m + b} \tag {4}
$$

One limitation to this approach that 宜 noted is the inherent assumption that the amount of diffuse light captured 将 constant throughout the entire range of climatic conditions that are being observed. This will certainly introduce noise into the measurements, but if sampling is high enough, the 线性回归分析（linear regression analysis） discussed above can provide a reasonable estimate for an average amount of diffuse capture that 可 used to better define the solar resource for such 聚光器（concentrator） PV 组件.

If the results observed present a clear inflection or break in the diffuse capture response behaviour of the CPV 组件, the regression analysis 可 split into multiple parts in a 分段方式. This could be a likely outcome given that the nature of diffuse light is quite variable in the relative amounts of circumsolar vs. isotropic diffuse light. By treating the 线性回归分析（linear regression analysis） in this fashion, one can determine the amount of diffuse capture ( $f_{d}$ ) as a function of a specific range of the $G_{i,b}/G_{i}$ ratio.

#### 8.3.11 聚光系统的日周测量

For 聚光系统, it 可 useful to measure circumsolar irradiance. Circumsolar irradiance is irradiance emanating from a region of the sky immediately surrounding the 太阳圆盘（solar disk）. Useful parameters to measure may include circumsolar contribution, circumsolar ratio, and sunshape. See ISO 9488.

The measured 直接法向辐照度（direct normal irradiance，DNI） (DNI) may include circumsolar contributions due to the 角接收范围（angular acceptance） of the DNI sensor. The fraction of measured DNI which is circumsolar 被定义 as the circumsolar ratio.

Concentrator systems may or may not be able to capture a portion of the circumsolar irradiance, depending on their design. However, measuring circumsolar quantities 可 useful for performance characterization purposes.

#### 8.3.12 辐照度的卫星遥感

卫星遥感 techniques use a dual approach to measuring the total surface 下行辐照度（downwelling irradiance） at the global horizontal plane. The on-board satellite instruments measure the radiance emitted or reflected by the earth's surface through the column of the atmosphere at specific visible and infrared spectral bands. The emitted radiance represents conditions where cloud cover is present, so measurements by this technique need to be framed in reference of the 晴空辐照度模型. Thus, the base of 卫星遥感 uses 辐射传输模型（radiative transfer models） to predict the clear sky condition, then satellite measurements are applied to the clear sky as reduction in irradiance due to 云不透明度（cloud opacity）.

Operating plants considering 卫星遥感辐照度 should consider the following when comparing to on-site measured irradiances. Validated sources of satellite irradiance will have documented reliability and consistency in terms of data availability and calibration, respectively. Because 卫星遥感辐照度 sources use a single set of carefully monitored on-board sensors covering entire continents at once, data 可 delivered with reduced uncertainty and cost associated with on-site maintenance, instrumentation soiling, 校准漂移 and location-to-location mismatches. The accuracy benefits of satellite remote sensed data come at different temporal and spatial averaging versus on-site measurements. Satellite measurement of 云不透明度（cloud opacity） occurs at the spatial scale determined by the resolution of the measurement hardware on the satellite. For most modern satellite networks, this is approximately $0,01^{\circ}$ by $0,01^{\circ}$ latitude or longitude (roughly 1 km by 1 km). Thus, the representation of the irradiance condition from satellite sources reflects the average irradiance over a $0,01^{\circ}$ by $0,01^{\circ}$ square area. In contrast, on-site measurements reflect irradiance conditions at the surface area of the sensor, effectively a single point. This difference in measurement area leads to differences in irradiance over various 时间平均 periods. Additionally, the satellite 图像采集频率 is typically 小于 ground hardware data logging. As a result of both effects, satellite and ground may show greater differences in irradiance measurements on the order of 10 % to 20 % for sub-hourly to hourly periods where plant operators 可 seeking analysis to diagnose plant underperformance. However, at monthly 最高至 yearly averaging periods, satellite and ground will align on the order of < 1 % to 5 %, where plant operators 可 seeking analysis to demonstrate overall plant performance.

Satellite remote sensed irradiances, including global horizontal, direct normal, diffuse, and in-plane irradiances are typically available in real time from 商业服务. Long-histories of satellite measurements 可 beneficial to plant operators as a reference for plant performance against 长期平均值/财务预测条件.

Important considerations when selecting satellite data are as follows:

- Satellite remote sensed irradiance data 宜 carefully selected after a review of accuracy and uncertainty.
- Satellite accuracy and uncertainty 宜 assessed against quality ground data from well-maintained sensors.
- Satellite sources 宜具有 a long history of measurement to verify accuracy across changes in satellite hardware.
- Satellite sources should provide data 最高至 current time, also for the purpose of evaluating accuracy.
- Satellite data 宜 版本化; e.g. the 元数据（metadata） about the satellite measurements 宜 traceable to a repeatable model basis.
- Satellite data should provide measurements at the 卫星硬件设备原生精度.
- The satellite remote sensed method 宜 specifically designed for measuring solar irradiance.

### 9.1 PV 组件温度

注 1 Cell junction temperatures are typically $1^{\circ}\mathrm{C}$ to $3^{\circ}\mathrm{C}$ higher than the temperature measured on the module's rear surface, depending on the module construction. 注 2 An 红外图像 of the front of the module may help confirm that the temperature of the 电池片 in front of the sensor is not substantially altered by the presence of the sensor or other factors.

Module temperature varies across each module and across the array. Temperature sensors 应 placed in 代表性位置 to capture the range of variation and allow determination of an 有效平均值.

### 9.2 环境空气温度

### 9.3 风速和风向

风速 and 风向 用于 estimating module temperatures. They may also be used for documenting warranty claims related to wind-driven damage.

### 9.4 污损比（SR）

Per 3.31, the soiling ratio is a property of the PV 阵列 cleanliness condition. 污损测量仪器 approximate the true soiling ratio of the PV 阵列 by measuring the impact of soiling on a 传感器表面 of the instrument and assuming that the soiling condition of the PV 阵列 is the same as that of the 传感器表面.

污损测量仪器 use various 物理原理:

- One measurement approach, which has many variations, compares a pair of PV 参考器件（PV reference device）s, one of which is routinely cleaned and the other of which soils naturally at the same rate as the PV 阵列. Methods for implementing this approach are described in detail in Annex C.
- Other approaches are 基于 optical principles, detecting soiling particles on a 采集表面 根据 their effect on either reflection or 光透射.

Some of the instrument types can measure the effect of 非均匀污损 on the power loss of PV 组件. Non-uniform soiling occurs when deposited soiling particles move under the influence of 露水、雨、风和重力, often collecting along PV 组件 edges, especially bottom edges. This can have a disproportionate effect on power, depending on module type. See Annex C.

### 9.5 降雨量

降雨量 measurements 可 used to estimate the cleanliness of modules. If soiling ratio is also measured, these data are complementary.

### 9.6 积雪

积雪fall measurements 可 used to estimate losses due to shading from 积雪. However, these losses may also be included in measurements of soiling ratio, depending on the soiling measurement device.

### 9.7 湿度

## 12 数据处理和质量检查

### 12.1 夜间

Night-time data may contain valuable information for 质量检查ing, 例如 总辐射表（pyranometer） and other instrumentation offsets. However, processed data for irradiance, PV-generated power, and other quantities expected to be zero at night 宜 set to zero during night-time after 质量检查s are performed, to avoid extraneous values.

### 12.2 质量检查

#### 12.2.1 剔除无效读数

The measured data 宜 checked and filtered, either automatically or manually, to identify missing or invalid data points and filter them out of subsequent analysis. Such missing or invalid data 宜 documented by the 监测系统.

Recommended 方法 of identifying missing or invalid data points include:

- applying physically reasonable minimum and maximum limits
- applying physically reasonable limits on maximum rates of change
- applying statistical tests to identify outlying values, including comparing measurements from multiple sensors
- applying contract data to identify viable parameter boundaries for certain performance data
• noting 错误代码 returned by sensors
• identifying and deleting redundant data entries
• identifying 缺失数据
• identifying readings stuck at a single value for an extended time
- checking 时间戳（timestamp）s to identify gaps or duplicates in data
- checking system availability reports.

#### 12.2.2 缺失数据处理

Missing or invalid data 可 treated in one of the following ways:

- the invalid or 缺失数据 可 replaced by values estimated from the valid data recorded before and/or after the invalid or 缺失数据;
- the invalid or 缺失数据 可 replaced with an average value for the analyzed interval;
- the data 可 treated in a manner specified in a valid contract, performance guarantee document, or other specification covering the installation;
- the analysed interval 可 treated as missing or invalid.

The treatment of missing or invalid data may depend on the goal of the measurement. For example, missing or invalid data associated with inverter issues 宜 discarded if the goal is strictly to quantify module performance, but 宜 retained if the goal is to capture all aspects of plant performance and availability.

Additional recommendations and requirements for treatment of missing or invalid data are included in IEC TS 61724-2 and IEC TS 61724-3.

The specific treatment of missing or invalid data 宜 documented in any reports.

### 13.2 求和

In the formulas given below involving summation, $\tau_{k}$ denotes the duration of the $k^{th}$ 记录间隔（recording interval） within a 报告周期（reporting period） (see Clause 6), and the symbol

$$
\sum_ {k}
$$

denotes summation over all 记录间隔（recording interval）s in the 报告周期（reporting period）.

注 that in formulas involving the product of power quantities with the 记录间隔（recording interval） $\tau_{k}$ the power 宜 expressed in kW and the 记录间隔（recording interval） in hours to obtain energy in units of kWh.

### 13.3 辐照量（irradiation）

Irradiation, also known as insolation, is the time integral of irradiance.

Each irradiation quantity H corresponding to an irradiance quantity G defined in Clause 3 被计算 by summing the irradiance as follows:

$$
H = \sum_ {k} G _ {k} \times \tau_ {k} \tag {5}
$$

For example, the 正面 in-plane or 正面 阵列平面（plane-of-array，POA） (POA) irradiation, $H_{i}$ , is given by:

$$
H _ {i} = \sum_ {k} G _ {i, k} \times \tau_ {k} \tag {6}
$$

and 背面 in-plane or 背面 阵列平面（plane-of-array，POA） (POA) irradiation, $H_{1}$ , is given by:

$$
H _ {i} ^ {\text {rear}} = \sum_ {k} \quad G _ {\mathrm{i,k}} ^ {\text {rear}} \times \tau_ {k} \tag {7}
$$

### 13.4 电能

#### 13.4.1 总则

Energy quantities 可 calculated from the integral of their corresponding measured power parameters over the 报告周期（reporting period）.

Alternatively, if power measurements are performed using sensors with built-in totalizers, the energy quantities 可 taken directly as measurement readings from the sensors.

#### 13.4.2 DC 输出电能

The PV 阵列 DC output energy is given by:

$$
E _ {\mathrm{A}} = \sum_ {k} P _ {\mathrm{A}, k} \times \tau_ {k} \tag {8}
$$

#### 13.4.3 AC 输出电能

The AC energy output is given by:

$$
E _ {\mathrm{out}} = \sum_ {k} F _ {\mathrm{out}, k} ^ {*} \times \tau_ {k} \tag {9}
$$

### 13.5 阵列额定功率

#### 13.5.1 DC 额定功率

The array DC 额定功率, $P_{0}$ , is the sum of the DC power output of all installed PV 组件 at the power rating reference condition, which is either:

– standard test conditions (STC), for monofacial and 双面组件（bifacial modules）; or
- 聚光器（concentrator） standard test conditions (CSTC), for 聚光系统. $P_{0}$ is given in units of kW.

$P_{0}$ 宜 calculated by using data from 制造商 datasheets or module labels, or, provided that the choice is specified, using alternative data 例如 laboratory or on-site test data.

The definition of $P_0$ that is used 宜 specified explicitly whenever quantities that depend on $P_0$ are reported.

注 that the definition of $P_0$ ignores any 背面 contribution for 双面组件（bifacial modules）. IEC 61215 includes provisions for measuring 双面组件（bifacial modules） using 背面 irradiance; however, these are provided for the purpose of indoor accelerated stress testing, not performance rating.

#### 13.5.2 AC 额定功率

The array AC 额定功率, $P_{0,AC}$ , is the lesser of:

- the array DC 额定功率 $P_0$ , and
– the sum of the inverter ratings in the system at specified operating temperature.

### 13.6 发电量折算值（yields）

#### 13.6.1 总则

发电量折算值（发电量折算值） are ratios of an energy quantity to the 阵列额定功率 $P_{0}$ . They indicate actual array operation relative to its rated capacity.

发电量折算值（发电量折算值） have units of $kWh \cdot kW^{-1}$ , where units of kWh in the numerator describe the energy production and units of kW in the denominator describe the system power rating. The ratio of units is equivalent to hours, and the 发电量折算值 ratio indicates the equivalent amount of time during which the array would be required to operate at $P_{0}$ to provide the particular energy quantity measured during the 报告周期（reporting period）.

#### 13.6.2 PV 阵列发电量折算值

The PV 阵列 energy 发电量折算值 $Y_{A}$ is the array energy output (DC) per rated kW (DC) of installed PV 阵列:

$$
Y _ {\mathrm{A}} = E _ {\mathrm{A}} / P _ {0} \tag {10}
$$

#### 13.6.3 最终系统发电量折算值

The final PV 系统 发电量折算值 $Y_{f}$ is the net energy output of the entire PV 系统 (AC) per rated kW (DC) of installed PV 阵列:

$$
Y _ {\mathrm{f}} = E _ {\text {out}} / P _ {0} \tag {11}
$$

#### 13.6.4 参考发电量折算值

The reference 发电量折算值 $Y_{r}$ for a monofacial PV 系统 可 calculated by dividing the total 正面 in-plane irradiation by the module's reference 阵列平面（plane-of-array，POA） irradiance:

$$
Y _ {\mathrm{r}} = H _ {\mathrm{i}} / G _ {\mathrm{i,ref}} \tag {12}
$$

where the reference 阵列平面（plane-of-array，POA） irradiance $G_{i,\mathrm{ref}}$ (kW·m $^{-2}$ ) is the irradiance at which $P_{0}$ is determined, usually defined under STC.

The reference 发电量折算值 represents the number of hours during which the solar radiation would need to be at reference irradiance levels 为了 contribute the same incident solar energy as was monitored during the 报告周期（reporting period） while the utility grid and/or 本地负载 were available.

If the 报告周期（reporting period） is 等于 one day, then $Y_{r}$ would be, in effect, the equivalent number of sun hours at the reference irradiance per day.

#### 13.6.5 双面参考发电量折算值

The reference 发电量折算值 $Y_{r}^{bl}$ for a bifacial PV 系统 可 calculated by taking the product of the 正面 in-plane irradiation and the 双面辐照度因子（bifacial irradiance factor，BIF） and dividing by the module's reference 阵列平面（plane-of-array，POA） irradiance:

$$
\mathrm{Y} _ {\mathrm{r}} ^ {\mathrm{b1}} = \sum_ {\mathrm{k}} \left(\mathrm{G} _ {\mathrm{i,k}} \times \tau_ {\mathrm{k}} \times \mathrm{BIF} _ {\mathrm{k}}\right) / \mathrm{G} _ {\mathrm{i,ref}} \tag {13}
$$

The reference 发电量折算值 represents the number of hours during which the solar radiation would need to be at reference irradiance levels 为了 contribute the same incident solar energy as was monitored during the 报告周期（reporting period） while the utility grid and/or 本地负载 were available.

If the 报告周期（reporting period） is 等于 one day, then $Y_{r}$ would be, in effect, the equivalent number of sun hours at the reference irradiance per day.

### 13.7 发电量折算损失

#### 13.7.1 总则

发电量折算损失 被计算 by subtracting 发电量折算值. The 发电量折算损失 also have units of kWh·kW $^{-1}$ (or h). They represent the amount of time the array would be required to operate at its rated power $P_{0}$ to provide for the respective losses during the 报告周期（reporting period）.

#### 13.7.2 阵列捕获损失

The array 捕获损失 $L_{c}$ represents the losses due to array operation, including losses in 布线 and junction boxes prior to DC measurement, array temperature effects, soiling, etc., and 被定义 as:

$$
L _ {\mathrm{C}} = Y _ {\mathrm{r}} - Y _ {\mathrm{A}} \tag {14}
$$

#### 13.7.3 系统平衡（Balance of systems，BOS）损失

The 系统平衡（balance of systems，BOS） (BOS) loss $L_{BOS}$ represents the losses in the BOS components, including the inverter and all 布线 and junction boxes not included in array 捕获损失, and 被定义 as:

$$
L _ {\mathrm{BOS}} = Y _ {\mathrm{A}} - Y _ {\mathrm{f}} \tag {15}
$$

### 13.8 效率

#### 13.8.1 阵列（DC）效率

The rated array 效率 is given by:

$$
\eta_ {\mathrm{A}, 0} = P _ {0} / (G _ {\mathrm{i,ref}} \times A _ {\mathrm{a}}) \tag {16}
$$

where the overall array area $A_{a}$ is the total module area, corresponding to the sum of the areas of the front surfaces of the PV 组件 as defined by their outer edges.

For a 聚光器（concentrator） module, if the front surface is not coplanar, the front surface 应 projected onto an appropriate two-dimensional surface to define the area.

The mean actual array 效率 over the 报告周期（reporting period） 被定义 by:

$$
\eta_ {\mathrm{A}} = E _ {\mathrm{A}} / (H _ {\mathrm{i}} \times A _ {\mathrm{a}}) \tag {17}
$$

#### 13.8.2 系统（AC）效率

The mean system 效率 over the 报告周期（reporting period） 被定义 by:

$$
\eta_ {\mathrm{f}} = E _ {\mathrm{out}} / (H _ {\mathrm{i}} \times A _ {\mathrm{a}}) \tag {18}
$$

Formula (18) can also be rewritten as:

$$
\eta_ {\mathrm{f}} = \eta_ {\mathrm{A}, 0} \times P R \tag {19}
$$

where

$\eta_{A,0}$ is the rated array 效率 defined in 13.8.1, and

PR is the 性能比（performance ratio） defined in 14.3.1.

#### 13.8.3 BOS 效率

The mean BOS 效率 over the 报告周期（reporting period） 被定义 by:

$$
\eta_ {\mathrm{BOS}} = E _ {\mathrm{out}} / E _ {\mathrm{A}} \tag {20}
$$

### 14.3 性能比（performance ratios）

#### 14.3.1 性能比（performance ratio）

The 性能比（performance ratio） PR is the quotient of the system's final 发电量折算值 $Y_{f}$ to its reference 发电量折算值 $Y_{r}$ , and indicates the overall effect of losses on the system. (Alternatively, the 性能比（performance ratio） 可 defined as a product of derate factors. See Annex D.) For monofacial PV 系统 it 被定义 as:

$$
P R = Y _ {\mathrm{f}} / Y _ {\mathrm{r}} \tag {21}
$$

$$
= (E _ {\mathrm{out}} / P _ {0}) / (H _ {\mathrm{i}} / G _ {\mathrm{i,ref}}) \tag {22}
$$

Expanding Formula (22) gives:

$$
P R = \left(\sum_ {k} \frac {P _ {\mathrm{out} , k} \times \tau_ {\mathrm{k}}}{P _ {0}}\right) / \left(\sum_ {k} \frac {G _ {\mathrm{i} , k} \times \tau_ {k}}{G _ {\mathrm{i,ref}}}\right) \tag {23}
$$

Both the numerator and denominator of Formula (23) have units of kWh·kW $^{-1}$ (or h). Moving $P_{0}$ to the denominator sum expresses both numerator and denominator in units of energy, giving PR as the ratio of measured energy to expected energy (基于ly on measured irradiance and neglecting other factors) over the given 报告周期（reporting period）:

$$
P R = \left(\sum_ {k} P _ {\text {out}, k} \times \tau_ {k}\right) / \left(\sum_ {k} \frac {P _ {0} \times G _ {\mathrm{i} , k} \times \tau_ {k}}{G _ {\mathrm{i,ref}}}\right) \tag {24}
$$

The annual 性能比（performance ratio）, $PR_{\mathrm{annual}}$ , is the 性能比（performance ratio） of Formula (24) evaluated for a 报告周期（reporting period） of one year.

注 1 The energy expectation expressed by the denominator of Formula (24) neglects the effect of array temperature, using the fixed value of 阵列额定功率, $P_{0}$ . Therefore, the 性能比（performance ratio） usually decreases with increasing irradiation during a 报告周期（reporting period）, even though energy production is increased, due to increasing PV 组件温度 which usually accompanies higher irradiation and results in lower 效率. This gives a seasonal variation, with higher PR values in winter and lower values in summer. It may also give geographic variations between systems installed in different climates.

注 2 Calculation of the 性能比（performance ratio） using GHI in place of in-plane (阵列平面（plane-of-array，POA）) irradiance $G_{i}$ is an alternative in situations where GHI measurements are available but $G_{i}$ measurements are not. In this case GHI is substituted for $G_{i}$ in Formula (24), resulting in a GHI 性能比（performance ratio）. The GHI 性能比（performance ratio） would typically show high values which may even exceed unity. The values cannot necessarily be used to compare one system to another, but 可 useful for tracking performance of a system over time and could also be applied to compare a system's measured, expected, and predicted performance using a performance model that is 基于ly on GHI.

#### 14.3.2 温度修正性能比

##### 14.3.2.1 总则

The seasonal variation of the 性能比（performance ratio） $PR$ of Formula (24) 可 significantly reduced by calculating a temperature-corrected 性能比（performance ratio） $PR'$ .

注 While variations in average 环境温度 are the most significant factor causing seasonal variations in measured 性能比（performance ratio）, other factors, 例如 seasonally dependent shading, spectral effects, and metastabilities can also contribute to the seasonal variation of PR.

##### 14.3.2.2 25 °C 性能比

The 25 °C 性能比（performance ratio）, $PR'_{25\,^\circ\text{C}}$ 被计算 by adjusting the power rating at each 记录间隔（recording interval） to compensate for differences between the actual PV 组件温度 and the STC reference temperature of 25 °C.

$PR_{25\,^{\circ}\mathrm{C}}$ 被计算 by introducing a power rating temperature adjustment factor $C_{k,25\,^{\circ}\mathrm{C}}$ into Formula (24), as follows:

$$
\mathrm{PR} _ {2 5 ^ {\circ} C} ^ {\prime} = \left(\sum_ {k} P _ {\mathrm{out}, k} \times \tau_ {k}\right) / \left(\sum_ {k} \frac {(C _ {k 2 5 ^ {\circ} C} \times P _ {0}) \times G _ {i , k} \times \tau_ {k}}{G _ {i , \mathrm{ref}}}\right) \tag {25}
$$

where $C_{k,25^{\circ}C}$ is given by:

$$
C _ {k, 2 5 ^ {\circ} \mathrm{C}} = 1 + \gamma \times (T _ {\mathrm{mod}, k} - 2 5 ^ {\circ} \mathrm{C}) \tag {26}
$$

Here $\gamma$ is the relative maximum-power 温度系数（temperature coefficient） (in units of $^{\circ}C^{-1}$ ), and $T_{mod,k}$ is the module temperature (in $^{\circ}C$ ) in time interval k.

With reference to Formula (26), $\gamma$ is typically negative, e.g. for crystalline silicon. The measured module temperature 可 used for $T_{\mathrm{mod},k}$ in Formula (26). However, if the monitoring objective is to compare $PR'_{25^{\circ}\mathrm{C}}$ to a target value associated with a performance guarantee, $T_{\mathrm{mod},k}$ should instead be estimated from the measured meteorological data with the same heat transfer model used by the simulation that set the performance guarantee value to avoid a bias error.

注 that Formulas (25) and (26) 可 used to calculate 性能比（performance ratio） adjusted to a different reference temperature by substitution of the desired reference temperature in Formula (26) in place of $25\ °C$ .

##### 14.3.2.3 年温度等效性能比

The annual-temperature-equivalent 性能比（performance ratio） $PR_{annual-eq}$ is constructed to approximate the annual 性能比（performance ratio） $PR_{annual}$ regardless of the duration of the 报告周期（reporting period）. It calculates the 性能比（performance ratio） during the 报告周期（reporting period） with the power rating at each 记录间隔（recording interval） adjusted to compensate for differences between the actual PV 组件温度 and an expected annual-average PV 组件温度. While this reduces seasonal variation in the metric, it does not remove the effect of annual-average temperature losses and leaves the value of the metric comparable to the value of $PR_{annual}$ .

$PR'_{annual-eq}$ 被计算 by introducing a power rating temperature adjustment factor $C_{k,annual}$ into Formula (24), as follows:

$$
P R _ {\text {annual - eq}} ^ {\prime} = \left(\sum_ {k} P _ {\text {out}, k} \times \tau_ {k}\right) / \left(\sum_ {k} \frac {\left(C _ {k , \text {annual}} \times P _ {0}\right) \times G _ {i , k} \times \tau_ {k}}{G _ {i , \text {ref}}}\right) \tag {27}
$$

where $C_{k,annual}$ is given by:

$$
C _ {k, \text {annual}} = 1 + \gamma \times (T _ {\mathrm{mod}, k} - T _ {\mathrm{mod,annual-avg}}) \tag {28}
$$

Here $\gamma$ is the relative maximum-power 温度系数（temperature coefficient） (in units of $^\circ \mathrm{C}^{-1}$ ), $T_{\mathrm{mod},k}$ is the PV 组件温度 in time interval $k$ , and $T_{\mathrm{mod,annual-avg}}$ is an annual-average module temperature.

注 With reference to Formula (28), $\gamma$ is typically negative, e.g. for crystalline silicon.

$T_{mod,annual-avg}$ is chosen 基于 historical weather data for the site and an empirical relation for the predicted module temperature as a function of ambient conditions and module construction. It 宜 calculated by computing an irradiance-weighted average of the predicted module temperature and then verified using the historical data for the site by confirming that the annual-equivalent 性能比（performance ratio） $PR'_{annual-eq}$ for the historical data (using Formulas (27) and (28)) is the same as the annual 性能比（performance ratio） $PR_{annual}$ for the historical data (using Formula (24)).

The measured module temperature 可 used for $T_{mod,k}$ in Formula (28). However, if the monitoring objective is to compare $PR_{annual-eq}$ to a target value associated with a performance guarantee, $T_{mod,k}$ should instead be estimated from the measured meteorological data with the same heat transfer model used by the simulation that set the performance guarantee value, to avoid a bias error.

#### 14.3.3 双面性能比

The monofacial 性能比（performance ratio） formulas presented above 可 transformed to bifacial 性能比（performance ratio） formulas by introducing the 双面辐照度因子（bifacial irradiance factor，BIF） (BIF) to correct the measured irradiance terms.

For example, the annual temperature-equivalent 性能比（performance ratio） for bifacial systems, $PR_{annual-eq.bi}^{\prime}$ 被计算 as for the monofacial equivalent in Formula (27) by multiplying the in-plane irradiance by the 双面辐照度因子（bifacial irradiance factor，BIF） (BIF) as follows:

$$
P R _ {\text {annual - eq,bi}} ^ {\prime} = \left(\sum_ {k} P _ {\text {out}, k} \times \tau_ {k}\right) / \left(\sum_ {k} \frac {\left(C _ {k , \text {annual}} \times P _ {0}\right) \times G _ {i , k} \times B I F _ {k} \times \tau_ {k}}{G _ {i , \text {ref}}}\right) \tag {29}
$$

where $C_{k,annual}$ is given by Formula (28). Just as this metric reduces seasonal variation due to temperature effects, it will also reduce seasonal variation due to changing albedo conditions (rain, 降雪, changes in vegetation, etc.).

Although only one example has been presented, note that the same correction could be applied to any other form of the 性能比（performance ratio）.

### 14.4 性能指数（performance indices）

A detailed performance model 可 used to predict 电气输出 of the PV 系统 as a function of meteorological conditions, known attributes of the system components and materials, and the system design. The performance model attempts to capture as precisely as possible all factors that can affect 电气输出.

In evaluating the system performance, particularly 相对于 a performance guarantee, it is desired to compare the measured output with the predicted and expected outputs. For a given 报告周期（reporting period）, the predicted output is the output calculated by the performance model when using historical weather data, while the expected output is the output calculated by the performance model when using measured weather data for the 报告周期（reporting period）.

The ratio of measured output to expected output for a given 报告周期（reporting period） defines a performance index. The performance index 可 evaluated either on the basis of power, defining power performance index PPI, or on the basis of energy, defining energy performance index, EPI.

The ratio of measured output to predicted output for a given 报告周期（reporting period） defines a baseline performance index. The baseline performance index 可 evaluated either on the basis of power, defining baseline power performance index BPPI, or on the basis of energy, defining baseline energy performance index BEPI.

For evaluation of a performance guarantee, the performance model used for calculation of expected power or expected energy 应 identical to the performance model used for calculation of predicted power or predicted energy used in the performance guarantee.

Further details on the application of a performance model to evaluate the model-based 性能指数（performance indices） are provided in IEC TS 61724-2 and IEC TS 61724-3.

## 附录 A（资料性）采样间隔（sampling interval）

### A.1 一般考虑

The 采样间隔（sampling interval） affects the quality of the 数据采集 process in representing the true signal. In determining 采样间隔（sampling interval）s and/or filtering 方法, the following factors 宜 considered:

- the 变化率 of the parameter to be measured;
- the rate of response of the measurement transducer;
- the treatment of the sampled data (例如 whether the data 将 used in further calculations that involve other sampled datasets, as is the case when calculating power from sampled current and voltage measurements); and
- the ultimate use of the sampled data and the desired limit of uncertainty in representing the true signal parameter.

### A.2 时间常数

In general, for rapidly changing signals, it is 推荐 that the 采样间隔（sampling interval） $(\tau_{s})$ be 小于 1/e (0,368) of the time constant of the measurement transducer, where the time-constant of a transducer is the time taken, after a step change in the measured variable, for the instrument to register 63.2 % of the step change in the measured parameter.

Alternatively, when the typical time constant of the measured parameter is longer than the time constant of the measurement transducer, the above requirement 可 relaxed. In this case the 采样间隔（sampling interval） need only be 小于 1/e of the measurement parameter time constant.

### A.3 混叠误差（aliasing error）

The 混叠误差（aliasing error） is the error associated with information lost by not taking a sufficient number of sampled data points. To avoid a large 混叠误差（aliasing error） the Nyquist sampling theorem suggests that a minimum of two samples per cycle of the data bandwidth 是必需的 to reproduce the sampled data with no loss of information.

For example, the Nyquist theorem suggests that if the highest frequency in the signal to be sampled is fmax, then the minimum sampling frequency would be 2·fmax. However, this sampling frequency still does not achieve a very accurate reproduction of the original signal (average error between the reconstructed signal and the original signal is 32 % at 2·fmax) and an increase in the sampling frequency to 200·fmax 是必需的 to achieve an accuracy of 1 % in the reconstructed signal.

An alternative option is to filter the signal before sampling. This is a very effective method of reducing the maximum frequency of the signal, but filtering also results in the loss of information. This is not an issue if the ultimate use of the data is to calculate simple averages over a period of time. However if the data 应 used in a calculation involving other sampled parameters (例如 the calculation of power from sampled voltage and current measurements) then analogue filtering before sampling removes fundamental elements of the time-dependent variation of the signal and can lead to the loss of accuracy in the calculated data.

### A.4 示例

As an example, consider the appropriate 采样间隔（sampling interval） for measurements of irradiance. The greatest fluctuations in the signal occur under 局部多云条件, as the irradiance sensor is alternately shaded and unshaded. Assume a worst-case situation in which the irradiance changes significantly due to passing clouds approximately once every 30 s. In addition, assume that the primary monitoring purpose is only to determine the average irradiance over a 报告周期（reporting period） of 1 h, rather than to recover the exact irradiance time series. In this case the 时间常数 are of more importance than the 混叠误差（aliasing error）. Sampling the irradiance 至少 once every 10 s 宜 adequate. For this example, a Monte Carlo simulation shows that the typical sampling-related uncertainty in the average irradiance recorded over 1 h is on the order of 0,5 %. This is negligible compared to typical instrumental uncertainty.

## 附录 B（资料性）组件温度传感器的选择和安装

### B.1 目的

This annex provides guidelines for 平板式（flat-plate） PV 组件 rear surface temperature measurement sensor selection and attachment in typical installed systems.

The sensor type and attachment method can have significant impacts on the measured temperature, leading to significant measurement errors. These errors are affected primarily by the contact between the sensor and the module's rear surface, the amount and type of insulation placed over the sensor, and the amount and type of 粘合剂（adhesive） used.

The recommendations stated in this annex are designed to minimize deviations from the ideal measurement condition while providing for secure and reliable long-term measurements.

### B.2 传感器和材料选择

#### B.2.1 推荐的传感器类型

Preference 宜 given to flat probes designed specifically for long-term surface measurements. Thin-film thermocouples of types T or E are generally acceptable. Small form-factor RTD and thermistor elements 可 utilized provided air gaps are minimized when applying the tape overlay. However, bead thermocouples, unpackaged resistive elements, and devices encased in cylindrical probe heads 宜 avoided when possible.

#### B.2.2 推荐的胶带

To minimize errors and to weather-proof the temperature sensor, reinforcement of the sensor and sensor leads is 推荐. This 可 accomplished by applying an 粘合剂（adhesive） overlay or tape.

Adhesive overlays and tapes 宜 fabricated from materials resistant to the effects of temperature, 湿度, and ultraviolet radiation. Avoid tapes not intended for use in securing sensors to surfaces – 例如 electrical tape, duct tape, aluminized cloth tape, foil tape, or packaging tape – as they 可 structurally weak and because their 粘合剂（adhesive）s tend to dry out over time or flow at elevated temperatures. Polyimide tapes (例如 Kapton) are known to be susceptible to embrittlement when exposed to ultraviolet radiation and moisture in the presence of oxygen (air) and 宜 avoided for long-term installations. Polyester is probably the most appropriate overlay material since many backsheets are constructed of multi-layer polyester and this material holds up well against moisture, temperature, and ultraviolet light. Pressure-sensitive silicone 粘合剂（adhesive） is generally applied to polyester tapes and is 推荐.

When using an overlay or tape, minimize air gaps as much as possible. Pockets of trapped air will temper the sensor response, thus negatively impacting the performance of the measurement system.

Temperature sensor readings 可 affected by wind, causing temperature readings lower than the 电池片 temperature. Application of thermally insulating tape over the sensor 可 used to suppress the wind cooling effect. For this purpose, using foam resin tape with an aluminium cover layer over the temperature sensor glued to the surface of the PV 组件 backsheet is introduced in IEC 60904-5.

#### B.2.3 氰基丙烯酸酯粘合剂（cyanoacrylate adhesives）与背板完整性

The use of cyanoacrylate 粘合剂（adhesive） on module backsheets 宜 avoided, because it is suggested by material 制造商s that cyanoacrylate may react chemically with PET (polyethylene terephthalate) or PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) backsheets, potentially resulting in the degradation of the 背板完整性（backsheet integrity） and thereby affecting the PV 组件's long-term encapsulation performance.

### B.3 传感器安装

#### B.3.1 永久安装与临时安装

Directions are provided for both 永久 and 临时 attachment.

Permanent attachment is 推荐 when long-term monitoring is desired and the sensor will not be removed or relocated. For instance, when including back-of-module temperature measurements within a fielded 数据采集 system.

Temporary attachment is 推荐 when the measurement sensor will need to be relocated or removed owing to the short-term nature of the monitoring, 例如 during commissioning or periodic maintenance.

#### B.3.2 安装位置

Select a sensor location at the centre of a 电池片 close to the centre of the module, avoiding boundaries between 电池片s.

For crystalline silicon modules, select the centre of the centre-most 电池片 within the module, or, when the module is built with even numbers of rows or columns of 电池片s, select one of the 电池片s closest to the centre.

For thin-film modules, place the sensor within the boundary of a 电池片 near the centre of the module, avoiding scribe lines between adjacent 电池片s if possible.

#### B.3.3 双面组件

For 双面组件（bifacial modules）, 背面 温度传感器 and 布线 shall 遮挡 $< 10\%$ of the area of any 电池片, and 布线 宜 routed in between 电池片s when possible.

#### B.3.4 方法

\- Clean the module's rear surface and sensor element of oil and dust by using lint-free wipes dampened with a $70\%$ solution of isopropyl alcohol in distilled water. Allow all cleaned surfaces to dry completely before proceeding.

\- Attach the sensor using the appropriate method:

a) Permanent:

- The 粘合剂（adhesive） 宜 confirmed to be compatible with the 背面表面 material so as to not affect the long-term integrity of the module.
- Mix a 导热（thermally conductive） epoxy as per 制造商 instructions.
- Apply the 粘合剂（adhesive） to the side of the sensor element intended to contact the module surface. Do not over-apply the 粘合剂（adhesive）; it 宜 as thin as possible yet fully coat the surface of the sensor element.
- Place the sensor element in the selected location. Manipulate the sensor to remove air bubbles and obtain a uniform 粘合剂（adhesive） thickness.

- Apply a polyester tape overlay to maintain the sensor position while the 粘合剂（adhesive） cures and to provide long-term protection of the sensor element. Round die-cut shapes are ideal as their lack of corners reduces the potential of delamination. If round shapes are not available, significantly round the corners of the tape using scissors.
- Allow the 粘合剂（adhesive） to cure as per the 制造商's instructions.

## 附录 C（规范性）使用清洁和污损 PV 参考器件对进行污损测量

### C.1 概述

This annex describes a method for measuring soiling losses by comparing two PV 参考器件（PV reference device）s, one of which is allowed to soil naturally at the same rate as the modules of the PV 阵列 and the other of which is routinely cleaned and serves as a reference.

### C.2 设备

Implementation of the method requires the following:

a) A reference PV 器件 (either monofacial device or 双面器件（bifacial device）), designated the "soiled" device, which is allowed to accumulate soiling at the same rate as the PV 阵列. The soiled device 可 either a PV reference 电池片 or PV 组件, but should preferably be a PV 组件 that is identical to or representative of those used in the PV 阵列 to be monitored so that it will soil at the same rate. It 应 mounted in the same plane as and at the average height of the PV 阵列, preferably with identical mounting mechanisms.
b) A reference PV 器件 (either monofacial device or 双面器件（bifacial device）), designated the "clean" device, which is regularly cleaned so that it is kept free of soiling. The clean device 可 either a PV reference 电池片 or PV 组件, but 应具有 similar spectral and angular response to the soiled device. The effect of any differences in response 宜 included in the 测量不确定度. The clean device 应 mounted close to the soiled device and co-planar to it within $0,5^{\circ}$ . Cleaning 可 performed either manually or by an automated system and 宜 done daily or 至少 once per week. For a 双面器件（bifacial device）, both the front side and rear side 应 cleaned.
c) A measurement system for measuring the 最大功率（maximum power） (method 1) and/or 短路电流（short-circuit current） (method 2) of the soiled device. Maximum power 可 measured using I-V curve tracing or max-power-point-tracking electronics.
d) A measurement system for measuring the 短路电流（short-circuit current） of the clean device.
e) A measurement system for measuring the temperatures of both the soiled and clean devices using 温度传感器 affixed to their rear surfaces.

For items c) and d), in between measurements, the measurement system 不应 hold the module in an electrical state which may cause degradation or metastable drift of the device. Therefore, typical crystalline silicon modules 宜 held at open-circuit (or max power) in between measurements, to avoid hot spot generation, while typical thin film modules 宜 held at short circuit (or max power) in between measurements. Observe the module 制造商's directions as needed to choose the appropriate hold state.

For 跟踪系统, the soiled and clean devices 应 mounted in the module plane of the tracker.

### C.3 归一化（normalization）

a) Choose a reference condition of irradiance and PV 器件 temperature, e.g. STC.
b) Determine a reference value for the 短路电流（short-circuit current） of the clean device at the designated reference condition. 它是 sufficient to use the 制造商's datasheet values. Additional measures may need to be considered for bifacial reference PV 器件.

c) Using the clean device to measure irradiance, determine reference values for the max power (method 1) and/or 短路电流（short-circuit current） (method 2) of the soiled device at the reference condition as follows:
- Completely clean the soiled device.
- Simultaneously measure the soiled device 最大功率（maximum power） and/or 短路电流（short-circuit current） and temperature 以及 the clean device 短路电流（short-circuit current） and temperature.
- Using the clean device measured 短路电流（short-circuit current） and temperature, with the reference data determined in step b), calculate the 有效辐照度（effective irradiance）.
- Using th被计算 irradiance and the measurements for the soiled device, calculate the 最大功率（maximum power） and/or 短路电流（short-circuit current） of the soiled device corrected to the reference condition of irradiance and temperature.

### C.4 测量方法 1 – 由污损引起的最大功率降低

Perform the measurement as follows:

a) Simultaneously (within 2 s) measure the 短路电流（short-circuit current） and temperature of the clean device and the max power and temperature of the soiled device.
b) Calculate the 有效辐照度（effective irradiance） from the values for the clean device measured in a), using the reference values determined in Clause C.3.
c) Calculate the expected max power of the soiled device at the irradiance determined in b) and the temperature measured in a), using the reference values determined in Clause C.3.
d) Calculate the soiling ratio $SR$ by dividing the soiled device max power measured in a) by its expected max power calculated in c).

### C.5 测量方法 2 – 由污损引起的短路电流降低

Perform the measurement as follows:

a) Simultaneously (within 2 s) measure the 短路电流（short-circuit current） and temperature of the clean device and the 短路电流（short-circuit current） and temperature of the soiled device.
b) Calculate the 有效辐照度（effective irradiance） from the values for the clean device measured in a), using the reference values determined in Clause C.3.
c) Calculate the expected 短路电流（short-circuit current） of the soiled device at the irradiance determined in b) and the temperature measured in a), using the reference values determined in Clause C.3.
d) Calculate the soiling ratio SR by dividing the soiled device 短路电流（short-circuit current） measured in a) by its expected 短路电流（short-circuit current） calculated in c).

### C.6 非均匀污损

Using a full-sized PV 组件 with Method 1 (max power reduction, Clause C.4) 发电量折算值 more accurate results because it best represents the actual power loss due to soiling, and in particular it produces more accurate results when soiling is non-uniform across the modules, especially for typical crystalline silicon modules. Method 2 (短路电流（short-circuit current） reduction) 可 adequate when soiling is assumed to be uniform across the modules or when the effects of soiling 非均匀性（non-uniformity） on the ratio of 最大功率（maximum power） to 短路电流（short-circuit current） are known to be small due to the construction or device physics of the module, e.g. for typical thin film modules. ### C.7 日平均值

The soiling ratio measured by the method described above is an instantaneous value. The instantaneously measured soiling ratio tends to show a time-of-day dependence due to residual angular 未对准 of the two 参考器件（reference devices） 以及 angle-dependent light scattering from soiling particles. Computation of daily average 可 performed either by:

a) averaging the instantaneously calculated soiling ratios over a daily period, or
b) summing the measured max power and expected max power (see Clause C.4) or measured 短路电流（short-circuit current） and expected 短路电流（short-circuit current） (see Clause C.5) over a daily period and calculating the ratio of the of the sums of measured to expected values

The number of data points passing the filter 宜 recorded as a quality metric and calculation of the daily average should only be performed when a sufficient number of data points are valid. The averaging 宜 irradiance-weighted. When angular 未对准 between the clean and soiled devices has been limited to $0.5^{\circ}$ , the averaging 宜包括 only times within $\pm2$ h of solar noon, on a fixed tilt system, or all times when solar 入射角（angle of incidence） is $<\sim35^{\circ}$ , on a tracking system. The 归一化（normalization） step in Clause C.3 应 repeated 至少每年一次.

Immediately following the 归一化（normalization） or following any significant 降雨量, the measured soiling ratio 宜 close to unity. Significant deviation from unity indicates a problem with the setup. This 可 used as a check of the 归一化（normalization）, so that the 归一化（normalization） 可 repeated if necessary.

## 附录 D（资料性）降额因子（derate factors）

Derate factors quantify individual sources of loss 相对于 the nameplate's DC 额定功率.

Derates 可 defined as a series of multiplicative factors contributing to the 性能比（performance ratio）, PR, 根据 the relation:

$$
P R = Y _ {\mathrm{f}} / Y _ {\mathrm{r}} = \prod_ {k = 1} ^ {N} D R _ {k} \tag {D.1}
$$

where the $DR_{k}$ are N individual derates corresponding to different loss mechanisms, and are given by:

$$
D R _ {k} = Y _ {k} / Y _ {k - 1} \tag {D.2}
$$

Here $Y_{k}$ is the system 发电量折算值 with loss mechanisms 1 through k operational, given by:

$$
Y _ {k} = Y _ {k - 1} - L _ {k} \tag {D.3}
$$

where $L_{k}$ is the 发电量折算值 loss due to loss mechanism k. $Y_{0}$ corresponds to $Y_{r}$ and $Y_{N}$ corresponds to $Y_{f}$ .

The number of derate factors 可 adjusted for different purposes, depending on the 系统规模 and analysis goals.

Categorizing all losses as either array capture or BOS losses, Formula (D.1) 可 written as:

$$
P R = D R _ {\text {capture}} \times D R _ {\text {BOS}} \tag {D.4}
$$

Here $DR_{capture}$ represents the combined array 捕获损失es, given by:

$$
D R _ {\text {capture}} = Y _ {\mathrm{A}} / Y _ {\mathrm{r}} = (Y _ {\mathrm{r}} - L _ {\mathrm{C}}) / Y _ {\mathrm{r}} \tag {D.5}
$$

and $DR_{BOS}$ represents the combined BOS losses, given by:

$$
D R _ {\mathrm{BOS}} = Y _ {\mathrm{f}} / Y _ {\mathrm{A}} = (Y _ {\mathrm{A}} - L _ {\mathrm{BOS}}) / Y _ {\mathrm{A}} \tag {D.6}
$$

As an aid to performance diagnosis, $DR_{capture}$ and $DR_{BOS}$ may each be rewritten as products of derates corresponding to individual contributing loss mechanisms within the capture and BOS categories. Determination of these contributing derate factors 可 done through direct measurement (例如, by measuring energies into and out of specific components of the system during the 报告周期（reporting period）, or by measuring specific loss mechanisms 例如 soiling) and/or modelling (例如, by fitting a performance model to the measured data within the 报告周期（reporting period）).
